Shipping Policy

Note: We ship live plants. To ensure they don't get stuck in transit over weekends, we typically dispatch orders from Monday to Wednesday.

1. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days.

2. Estimated Delivery Time

Delivery usually takes between 3 to 7 business days depending on your location.

  • Metro Cities: 3-4 Days
  • Rest of India: 5-7 Days

3. Packaging

We use scientifically designed ventilated corrugated boxes. Plants are hydrated and secured with coco-peat to ensure they remain moist during transit.

4. Serviceable Areas

We currently ship to 15,000+ pin codes across India via our courier partners (Delhivery, Bluedart, etc.). If your area is unserviceable, we will refund your amount immediately.
