Who We Are

Rooted in Nature, Growing with Care.

Welcome to New Modern Himalayan Nursery. Established in the lush foothills, we are more than just a plant nursery; we are a community of nature enthusiasts dedicated to bringing the serenity of the Himalayas to your doorstep.

With over 10 years of experience in horticulture, we specialize in acclimatizing exotic and native species for your home and garden. Whether you are a seasoned gardener or a budding plant parent, our team ensures that every sapling you take home is healthy, resilient, and ready to thrive.

Our Purpose

Guiding principles that drive our growth.

Our Mission

To cultivate a greener world by providing high-quality, sustainable plant varieties while educating our community on the importance of ecological balance and organic gardening practices.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted botanical haven in the region, where every household embraces the joy of gardening, creating breathable, vibrant living spaces for future generations.
