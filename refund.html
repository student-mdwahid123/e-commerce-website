Return & Refund Policy

1. The "Green Guarantee"

We take great care in packing. However, if your plant arrives in a damaged or dead condition, we offer a Free Replacement..

2. How to Claim Free Replacement?

You must inform us within 24 hours of delivery.

  1. Take clear photos/videos of the damaged plant and the box.
  2. Email them to support@greenhaven.com or WhatsApp us at +91-9876543210.
  3. Our team will review the issue and process your refund within 3-5 business days.
Important: Minor leaf shedding or yellowing due to transit stress is normal. With water and sunlight, the plant will bounce back. Such cases do not qualify for a refund.

3. Cancellations

You can cancel your order before it is dispatched (usually within 12 hours). Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled.

4. Returns

Since plants are perishable goods, we do not accept returns. If you are unhappy with the product, please contact us for a resolution instead of sending the plant back.
